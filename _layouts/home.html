About Me

I have a degree in Computer Science and Psychology from the University of Toronto. I currently work at BiblioCommons -- a SaaS company that works with public libraries across the U.S. and Canada to deliver a seamless online library experience. Before this, I worked at WaveDNA -- A music technology start up in Toronto responsible for creating an innovative suite of tools and software to help musicians make great music while speeding up their workflow. I'm a full stack developer with a wide set of interests, and I love integrating those interests with my work.

In my free time, I enjoy music, reading, cycling, and rock climbing. Oh, and puppies!
